Directions: Kung Fu Pogo Fighter is a multiplayer fighting game. Two players fight each other until one of their health bars reaches zero. Player 1 uses W, A and D to move, S to throw shurikens... Player 2 uses the arrow keys in the same functionality, with Up, Left, and Right moving the character, Down throwing shruikens. The mouse is used in menus to select objects such as start buttons or map selection tiles.
